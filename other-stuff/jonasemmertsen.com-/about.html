Biography Biography

For the past 10 years, I've worked as an Art Director & Creative Director with some of the top talent, agencies, and clients in the digital space. My expertise lies in Digital Design, and I love getting hands-on. I'm a multidisciplinary jack-of-all-trades, with a skill set that ranges from conceptual thinking to creating high-quality designs.
