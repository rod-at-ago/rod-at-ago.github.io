Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect information such as visitor log information or visitor behavior information. When you visit the Website, information from or about you may be collected through cookies or similar technology.

Session cookies are temporary cookies that are used to remember you during the course of your visit to the Website, and they expire when you close the web browser.

Persistent cookies are used to remember your preferences within the Website and remain on your desktop or mobile device even after you close your browser or restart your computer. Ecopia uses these cookies to analyze user behavior to establish visit patterns to improve the Website’s functionality for you and others who visit the Website. These cookies also allow Ecopia to serve you with targeted advertising and measure the effectiveness of the Website’s functionality and advertising.
