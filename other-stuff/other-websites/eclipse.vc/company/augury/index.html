Augury

Augury was founded in 2011 by Saar Yoskovitz and Gal Shaul to build a world where people can always rely on the machines that matter, by providing our customers with superior insights into the health and performance of the machines they use to make products, deliver services, and improve lives.

Augury’s end-to-end solutions help eliminate production downtime, improve process efficiency, maximize yield, and reduce waste and emissions. They operate in a variety of manufacturing sectors, such as food, beverage, consumer packaged goods, pulp and paper, forest products, chemicals and pharmaceuticals, as well as in the energy market.

They recently acquired process health leader Seebo to unlock Production Health for the industry by providing AI-driven insights into the interdependence of machines, processes, and operations. By leveraging these insights, businesses will make better decisions, improve outcomes, empower the workforce, and benefit the environment.

Eclipse Carbon Optimization Framework

Augury, which offers 'machine health,' a physical and digital service that ensures higher uptime, more efficient energy use, and higher yield from machinery, cuts emissions by 13%, resulting in 2.2 MMtCO2e reduced annually by 2040.
