The Project

The idea for luxury fashion brand ENCOMMÚN Official was born after their co-founders Deb Barton and Lisa Liberati met on a project with filmmaker M. Night Shyamalan. The brand features a conceptual women’s wear capsule line based on expanding the narrative of fashion as an art form. Each collection is inspired and designed in dialogue with art to further the conversation.

I worked with their talented designer to build the brand’s first ecommerce experience. My role was to turn the design into a fully functional Shopify store. I built a website that does a really nice job showcasing beautiful photography and unique products, with performance and flexibility under the hood. Powered by Shopify, the website is easy to manage and update.
