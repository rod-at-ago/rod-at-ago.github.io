about me

Hi! My name is Ira.

I am a video and photo creator from Ukraine. I started my path with filming as a self-learner. Capturing moments and seeing how they keep on living on the screen — is magic for me. Later I began to make photoshoots for people, brands, and different projects. And that's the way I became a video and photo creator.

My biggest inspiration is the diversity of people and the world around us and how everything is connected. I traveled a lot and lived for a while on the island, and this experience certainly influenced my work.

I am an open-minded person, a life explorer, and a traveler. I believe that there are no limits and that everything is possible. We are all creators inside.

In the creative process, my core values are trust, personal approach, understanding, and quality.
